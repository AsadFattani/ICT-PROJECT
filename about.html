About Speedy Paws


Welcome to Speedy Paws, where speed meets paws (no, we don’t mean actual paws, though they’d be adorable)! This project is proudly built as part of our ICT curriculum, designed to showcase our skills in creating a responsive and interactive web application.


Our mission? To bring you a lightning-fast, smooth, and enjoyable experience as you interact with our platform. Whether you’re here to challenge your typing skills or just to explore, we hope you’ll have a blast!


About Us
